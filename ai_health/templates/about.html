ℹ About AI Based Health Monitoring System

Project Overview

The AI Based Health Monitoring System is an intelligent healthcare application developed to assist users in monitoring their overall health condition using Artificial Intelligence. The system collects basic health parameters from the user and performs health analysis to estimate the risk levels of major diseases such as Heart Disease, Diabetes, Kidney Disease, and Stroke.

Project Objectives

  • Provide a simple and user-friendly health monitoring platform.
  • Predict the risk of multiple diseases using AI-based analysis.
  • Generate an overall health report instantly.
  • Provide personalized health recommendations.
  • Help users become more aware of their health conditions.

How the System Works

  1. User registers and logs into the system.
  2. User enters basic health details such as age, heart rate, blood pressure, temperature, oxygen level, and blood sugar.
  3. The AI engine analyzes the entered health parameters.
  4. The system predicts the risk level of Heart Disease, Diabetes, Kidney Disease, and Stroke.
  5. An overall health report and personalized recommendations are displayed.

Main Features

  • 🤖 AI-Based Health Analysis
  • ❤️ Heart Disease Risk Prediction
  • 🩸 Diabetes Risk Prediction
  • 🩺 Kidney Disease Risk Prediction
  • 🧠 Stroke Risk Prediction
  • 📊 Overall Health Score
  • 📈 Health History
  • 💡 Personalized Health Tips
  • 🚑 Emergency Contacts
  • 📄 Download Health Report

Benefits

  • Early identification of potential health risks.
  • Quick and easy health assessment.
  • Improves health awareness.
  • Supports preventive healthcare.
  • Easy to use for people of all age groups.
"AI cannot replace doctors, but it can assist users by providing early health risk assessment and encouraging timely medical consultation."
